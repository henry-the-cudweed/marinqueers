We are a small community of queer/trans people in Marin County, California, and here you will learn about our events and projects, along with resources.

Marin Queers support group meets every second and fourth Thursday of the month, 7pm-8:30pm. We are currently meeting on Zoom, and we will post an update if there are any changes.

Marin Queers is a peer led support group for queer, trans and questioning folx living in Marin to gather, support one another and build community. If you would like to join or ask any questions, please email marinqueers@gmail.com, or send a message to marinqueers on Instagram.
